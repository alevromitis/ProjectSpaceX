About SpaceX

Founded in 2002 by Elon Musk, SpaceX (Space Exploration Technologies Corp.) is a pioneering aerospace manufacturer and space transportation company based in Hawthorne, California. Driven by a bold vision to revolutionize space technology and make human life multi-planetary, SpaceX is dedicated to reducing the cost of space travel and ultimately enabling the colonization of Mars.
